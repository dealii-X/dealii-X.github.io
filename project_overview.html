Overview

dealii-X is a modular collection of libraries and applications for developing high-performance organ simulators built on top of deal.II.

  • Purpose: enable development and sharing of reusable components for organ simulations (brain, heart, lungs, liver, mechanobiology).

  • Audience: researchers and developers integrating deal.II with solvers, preconditioners, and multi-physics coupling tools.

See the technical scope at technical_scope.md.
